Choux Cream

Descripton

These homemade choux creams are made with a light choux pastry that puffs up when baked. In this version, they are filled with a creme patissiere that's easy to make and is on top of the recipes list. You can add melted chocolate on top for a much better experience.

Ingredients

  • 1 recipe of cream Patissiere
  • ½ cup butter
  • 1 cup water
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 bar melted chocolate (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  2. In a large pot, bring water and butter to a rolling boil.
  3. Stir in flour and salt until the mixture forms a ball.
  4. Transfer the dough to a large mixing bowl. Using a wooden spoon or stand mixer, beat in the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each.
  5. Drop by tablespoonfuls onto an ungreased baking sheet, spacing them a couple of inches apart.
  6. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes in the preheated oven, until golden brown. Centers should be dry.
  7. When the shells are cool, either split and fill them with the pudding mixture, or use a pastry bag to pipe the creme patissiere into the shells.
  8. Dip the top of the choux cream in melted chocolate (optional)
