Tiramisu

Descripton

Tiramisu is a classic Italian layered dessert with rum-flavored, coffee-soaked ladyfingers, a creme patissiere, and whipped cream. This tiramisu recipe is easy to make at home for an impressive no-bake dessert.

Ingredients

  • 1 recipe of creme patissiere
  • 1 ¼ cups heavy cream
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ cup strong brewed coffee, at room temperature
  • 2 tablespoons rum
  • 2 (3 ounce) packages ladyfinger cookies
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Beat cream and vanilla in a medium bowl with an electric mixer until stiff peaks form.
  2. Combine coffee and rum in a small bowl.
  3. Split ladyfingers in half lengthwise and drizzle with the coffee mixture.
  4. Arrange 1/2 of the soaked ladyfingers in the bottom of a 7x11-inch dish.
  5. Spread 1/2 of the creme patissiere over the ladyfingers, then spread 1/2 of the whipped cream over top. Repeat layers once more.
  6. Sprinkle cocoa powder over top.
  7. Cover and refrigerate until set, 4 to 6 hours.
